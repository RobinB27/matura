algorithm SimpleSentiment strategy

    previousSentimentScore = 0
    threshold = user-defined integer

    for every iteration:
        for every stock in Portfolio:
            currentSentimentScore = getCurrentSentimentScore()

            scoreDifference = currentSentimentScore - previousSentimentScore

            if scoreDifference > threshold:
                buy Stock
            else if scoreDifference < -threshold:
                sell Stock
            else:
                ignore Stock
            
            previousSentimentScore = currentSentimentScore
